There is a major problem concerning people using their phones while driving. It's a dangerous distraction to the people who are driving. They do not pay as much attention to the road as they should and could be involved in an accident. The rising use of social media also adds to the rapid use of phones while driving.

The vast number of phone users cause concern for other drivers. Although others critize they also are guilty of using phones while driving themselves. It's such a rising problem that laws have been set allowing officers to ticket drivers who text and drive.

Certain vehicles prevent the operation of the phone while running. While only a few vehicles do this, many people are still able to text and drive. The scarcity of these cars do not create the significant impact needed to reduce the number of accidents caused by phone use while driving

Proposing to create an app that installs on mobile devices, it will connect via bluetooth to a device that plugs into vehicles.Driving Mode

The physical part of the device just plugs into the car and monitors the speed of the vehicle. After the vehicle reaches a certain speed, this device will intercept the signal of the phone. It basically takes on the number of the phone. This device will prevent the phone from sending and receiving calls and text messages while the vehicle is in operation.

Device will monitor the speed to sense motion. Then triggers signal that will intercept users phone signal. See for yourself.

Creating an app to cut signal temorarily while in motion will reduce phone related accidents. Although it will not eliminate phone use entirely, once the app and device come into use it will decrease usage of phones while in motion.

Device will send automatic reply to any incoming calls or texts. "Your friend _____ is busy driving"

Message will be sent that the user is busy driving and will answer when they've reached a stop.The device will store information, such as phone numbers and texts, inside the circuitry until the vehicle is not motion. When stopped the information missed will be sent to the users phone. Once the vehicle continues motion, phone will be disabled again. Security in the device will prevent third party programs from getting information stored on it.
